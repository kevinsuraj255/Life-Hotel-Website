Welcome To

Life Hotel New York

Once the legendary home of Life Magazine, Life Hotel NYC now stands as one of Manhattan’s most distinctive boutique stays. Set in the heart of NoMad, the hotel blends historic architecture, editorial heritage, and contemporary comfort across a thoughtfully curated guest experience.

Blue, white, gold, and black define the visual language of the property, reflecting a balance of timeless New York glamour and modern refinement.

History

From Publishing Landmark to Boutique Destination

Designed in the Beaux-Arts tradition, the building at 19 West 31st Street carries a remarkable legacy in New York City history. It once served as the first home of Life Magazine, where writers, editors, artists, and cultural tastemakers helped shape an era of American storytelling.

Today, Life Hotel preserves that spirit through rich design, layered details, and an atmosphere that feels distinctly editorial, creative, and unmistakably New York.
